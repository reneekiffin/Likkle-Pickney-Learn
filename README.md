# Likkle-Pickney-Learn
Jamaican heritage for children — heroes, patois, parishes, and wildlife in one playful app.
Likkle Pickney Learn is a joyful celebration of Jamaican culture, 
designed specifically for children ages 2 to 11+.

🇯🇲 WHAT YOUR CHILD WILL LEARN

- Our 7 National Heroes — Marcus Garvey, Paul Bogle, Nanny of the Maroons, 
  and more, with real stories and fun facts
- National Symbols — the flag, coat of arms, ackee, doctor bird, and 
  the Jamaican motto "Out of Many, One People"
- The Jamaican Alphabet — A is for Ackee, B is for Breadfruit, 
  X is for Xaymaca (our original Taino name!)
- Patois Dictionary — over 130 words with meanings and examples. 
  Wah gwaan? Pickney? Boonoonoonoos? Find out what they all mean.
- Our Native Animals — the Doctor Bird, Jamaican Iguana, Giant 
  Swallowtail Butterfly, and more endemic creatures
- Jamaican Proverbs — wisdom from our elders with fun quizzes
- The 14 Parishes — explore an interactive map of Jamaica
- National Anthem & Pledge — every Jamaican child should know them

🎓 AGE-APPROPRIATE CONTENT
Content automatically adjusts for toddlers, early learners, 
middle-age kids, and older children.

🎮 FUN GAMES & QUIZZES
- Swipe through beautiful picture cards
- Multiple-choice quizzes for heroes, proverbs, and animals
- Matching games for letters, meanings, and photos
- Score tracking and trophy rewards

📱 DESIGNED FOR KIDS
- Large, easy-to-tap buttons
- Bright, friendly design
- No ads, no in-app purchases, no tracking
- Works offline — take it anywhere
- Parents and teachers can easily add their own content

Built with love for Jamaican children at home and across the diaspora. 
Perfect for families wanting to pass down our beautiful culture to 
the next generation.
